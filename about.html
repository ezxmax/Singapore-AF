About Us

The Singapore Air Force (RSAF) is the air arm of the Singapore Armed Forces. It was first established in 1968 as the Singapore Air Defence Command (SADC).

Today, the RSAF has a network of well-equipped airbases, maintenance facilities, and training institutes to ensure operational readiness.
